While this number is large, it has halved this century, falling from 1.35 billion to 675 million. You can see this in the chart.However, this progress has been far from even. The number has fallen across all regions except Sub-Saharan Africa, where it has increased.That doesn’t mean no progress has been made: the share of people in Sub-Saharan Africa with electricity has doubled, rising from 26% to 53%.
