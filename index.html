In the world outside of China, 33% lived in extreme poverty in 1990; by 2025, this share was down to 12%.The large economic growth that lifted 940 million Chinese people out of extreme poverty since 1990 was a major contributor to the global decline in poverty. But the non-Chinese world also achieved a very large reduction.It is not true that the global decline in poverty was only due to China.
