In 2000, Asia was home to most of the world’s population living in extreme poverty. However, strong economic growth in recent decades has led to steep reductions in poverty. Progress in Sub-Saharan Africa has been much slower.If we're serious about ending extreme poverty worldwide, supporting economic development in Sub-Saharan Africa has to be a top priority.
