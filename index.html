Since 2000, nuclear and renewables have followed very different trajectories. Back then, both categories made up a similar share of global electricity, but today, renewables make up more than three times as much: 30% compared to 9%.The total amount of electricity produced by nuclear plants is almost exactly the same as it was two decades ago.
