Internet access has grown rapidly over the last few decades. This is also changing quickly.In 2015, less than half — 44% — of the world population was in the range of a 4G network. That figure is now 90%. In the chart, you can see that this has increased quickly across many regions. This data comes from the International Telecommunication Union.Note that being in the range of a 4G network doesn’t mean that someone is using it.
